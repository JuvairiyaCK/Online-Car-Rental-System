ABOUT THE CAR RENTAL SYSTEM

A car rental, hire car or car hire agency is a company that rents automobiles for short periods of time to the public, generally ranging from a few hours to a few weeks. It is often organized with numerous local branches (which allow a user to return a vehicle to a different location), and primarily located near airports or busy city areas and often complemented by a website allowing online reservations. Car rental agencies primarily serve people who require a temporary vehicle, for example, those who do not own their own car who are out of town, or owners of damaged or destroyed vehicles who are awaiting repair or insurance compensation. Car rental companies provide a fleet of vehicles that the customers can choose from based on their needs, like size, model, and duration of use. The rental agreement includes the terms regarding the rental period, mileage limitations, fuel responsibilities, and insurance coverage.
